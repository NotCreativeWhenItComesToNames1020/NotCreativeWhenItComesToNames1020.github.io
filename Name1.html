New Year's Resolutions

When the sun set on January the 3rd, I thought about what 2018 would hold, whether it be positive or negative. Also, as usual in the beginning of the year, I began to think up of some resolutions for myself. First I thought of the more common one; losing some weight. I was a little overweight, so that was one. Then, I wanted at least one more, so I thought of this one: trying to do better in geometry. In the second marking period, I got a 70, which was passing, but not what i wanted. I knew i could do better, so that was my other resolution. Wish me luck!
